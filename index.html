Last Updated 7/1/21

This is a project I finished recently. I've been wanting to experiment with different body types and I've been watching a lot of @LeanBeefPatty on TikTok so I thought it would be fun to draw a muscular female character.

Often when I begin doing a "for-fun" drawing like this it usually just begins with me drawing something random on the canvas and adjusting it till It fit's my liking. In this sketch you can see me roughly trying to play around with breaking down the figure into 3D shapes. This is something I hope to find more practice with in the future.

The next step was line-art. This is an image of an old piece from a month-ish ago. I've noticed recently I've begun slacking when I create line art so when I went into this piece, I wanted to put explicit focus on this to try and deliver something that looks more up to standard.

The line-art I ended up producing felt much better to me then the older drawing. The lines felt more intentional like I was taking my time and giving it my attention.
